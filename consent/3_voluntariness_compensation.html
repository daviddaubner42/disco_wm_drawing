Participant Information

Voluntariness of Participation

Participation in this study is voluntary. You can withdraw your participation at any time without stating any reason and without any adverse consequences. During the experiment, you can just close the browser window if you want to stop the study. If you want your data to be deleted please let one of the supervising experimenters know.
You may stop the experiment and revoke the declaration of consent at any time.

You have the right to object to the further processing of the data provided by you and to request its deletion. In these cases, please contact the project manager or researcher. You will find names and contact details at the end of this form. Please note, however, that it is no longer possible to delete the experimental data collected from you after it has been anonymized (see declaration of consent, section "Data protection").

Benefits for the Public

This study may increase our understanding of human cognitive processes and lays the foundation for neurocognitive investigations in the future.
