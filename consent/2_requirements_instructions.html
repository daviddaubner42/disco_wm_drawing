Participant Information

Requirements and Instructions

You will receive detailed instructions at the beginning of the experiment. Here's a brief summary:

During the experimental part of the study, you will be presented with a picture of a random shape. After a long delay, you will be asked to recall the shape by drawing it using a drawing tablet. In the end, you will fill out a short questionnaire about your experience with the task.

Please concentrate and work conscientiously on the task. Please don't press the reload button of your browser, this will prevent you from continuing the experiment.
