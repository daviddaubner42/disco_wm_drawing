Participant Information

Dear Participant,

Thank you for your interest in our study! In what follows, we would like to inform you about the purpose and process of the study and explain everything you need to know about participation and data protection regulations. If you have any questions, please contact the experimenter.

Purpose and Duration of the Experiment

The purpose of the study is to gain knowledge about the memorization of visual stimuli. You will be presented with different shapes, which you have to memorize and then draw from memory. This experiment comprises one session of approx. 120 minutes.
